20 years of IEEE FISAT SB

  • img3()

This year, IEEE FISAT SB will commemorate its 20th anniversary As part of the celebrations, the Execom Members of IEEE FISAT SB are planning to host around 20 days of events for school and college students and the community. These two weeks of events will comprise our signature events IMPULSE and DROID as well as our outreach programs RIDE and a series of programs for the Neurodivergent (Differently abled) population called EDEN. There will also be an industrial expo OPTIMUS 2.0, which will be Kerala's largest Robotics Startup Expo organized by an educational institution. P-HACK, a humanitarian activity and SKILLDIVE,the placement training program are the other two major events conducted as part of the celebrations.

REGISTRATIONS OPENS SOON




IMPULSE 4.0

IMPULSE 4.0, the fourth version of the flagship event of the IEEE Student Branch of FISAT will be hosted as a three-day National Event on the 14th, 15th, and 16th of April 2023.

The Techno-Managerial conclave will include technical workshop sessions and invited speakers' sessions along with ice-breaking sessions.

Go To Impulse

DROID 3.0

DROID 3.0 is a 3-day national conclave on robotics that focuses to enhance the knowledge and learning of young minds in the field of robotics and automation

It is being held as the third iteration of DROID 19, a successful event that took place in the year 2019 and resulted in a significant triumph. It comprises Distinguished Lecture programs, various industrial talk sessions, a 12-hour hackathon, expos, and workshops.

Go To Droid

EDEN

FISAT in collaboration with Inclusys Org Foundation will conduct training sessions for neurodivergent students at different empowerment centers across the district

The neurodivergent students will be trained on Google AI experiments. The best performers will be invited for a small competition at FISAT and the Winners will be awarded during the DROID 3.0 Inauguration ceremony.

PHACK

PHACK will be conducted in selected villages. The project will focus on providing basic electrical and power-related facilities to economically weaker villages that cannot afford these basic facilities

Repair work and the supply of energy-efficient equipment will be performed by well-trained students from the college, giving them real-world work experience.

REGISTER FOR PHACK

RIDE 2.0

RIDE is a humble initiative to provide imperative training on state-of-the-art technology, which stems from our innate desire to provide better education for the less fortunate. The RIDE program offers essential technology training to underprivileged students to help them adapt and grow in a rapidly changing world. Programming is a highly sought-after skill that can lead to employment opportunities, and robotics prepares students for the future workforce.

The program will include an ice-breaking session and fundamental sessions in programming and robotics. One of the first session of RIDE was conducted at Adoor Pathanamthitta which introduced large groups of students to the future development technologies.Students will gain valuable skills and knowledge that they would not have had otherwise through this combined training program.

REGISTER FOR RIDE 2.0

OPTIMUS 2.0

Optimus 2.0 is Kerala's largest Robotics industrial Startup Expo organized by IEEE FISAT SB with IEEE Robotics and Automation Society FISAT under the event DROID 3.0. The Expo will feature a variety of exhibits and workshops, including five demonstrations of the exhibiting company’s latest products and technologies.

This includes various all India levels of companies to make up the event remarkable and sensational. This event will be an inspiration to the young entrepreneurs who are yet to set up their own industry. This event can be interesting for those who are into robotics and for those who love to be into expert systems and machine learning. It would be an innovative occasion to explore the various companies.
